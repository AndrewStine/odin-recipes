Chicken Quesadillas

Description

This chicken taco recipe is an easy one for those who don't like to spend a lot of time in the kitchen after a day of work. Most of the work will take place in your crockpot during the day and will be ready once you get home. This is also great for leftovers. I usually make enough to last a couple days. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Salsa - 1 Cups
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chilis in Adobo - 1 Can
  • Large Tortillas

Directions

  1. Salt and pepper chicken breasts to taste
  2. Chop up chilis in adobo into small pieces
  3. Place chicken breasts in crockpot with 1 cup of salsa and chopped chilis in adobo and cook on low for 8 hours
  4. After 8 hours, turn off crockpot and shred chicken inside the crockpot using two forks
  5. Add 1 TSP of olive oil to frying pan and heat on medium-high
  6. Add layer of cheddar cheese and chicken to tortilla in pan
  7. Place top tortilla on chicken and cheese and fry and flip until quesadilla is cooked through
