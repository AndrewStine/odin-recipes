Chicken and Spinach Pizza

Description

I could eat pizza everyday I think. That is why I love this recipe. It is simple yet gives you the satisfaction of a home cooked meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Naan Flatbreads - 2
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spinach
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  2. Salt and pepper chicken breasts to taste
  3. Heat 1 TBSP of olive oil in a pan on medium high heat
  4. Cook chicken 6-7 minutes on each side
  5. Remove chicken and cut into think slices
  6. Spread thin layer of olive oil on naan flatbreads
  7. Add italian seasoning to naan and salt/pepper to taste
  8. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese to naan and top with chicken slices and spinach
  9. Bake pizza for about 10 minutes
  10. Remove from oven, cut, and serve!
